Frequently Asked

Do I need to be home during the service? +

Nope! As long as we have access to your yard, our crew can complete the service while you're out. We'll send you a completion notification with photos when we're done.

What if it rains on my scheduled day? +

We monitor weather closely and will reschedule to the next available dry day at no extra charge. You'll always be notified in advance.

Can I cancel or pause my plan? +

Absolutely. All monthly plans are cancel-anytime with no penalty. Just give us 5 days notice before your next billing date.

Do you provide equipment and supplies? +

Yes — our team brings everything needed. You don't need to supply any tools, fertilisers, or bags. We handle it all.
